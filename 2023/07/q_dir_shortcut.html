    Q-Dir makes your files and folders easy to manage. Quick and easy access, with an amazing technique based on a quadruple view.

    You don’t have to give up the usual Drag and Drop, all Views, and other features of your system.
    Year! Q-Dir gives you other nice features, which will make you happy.
    Many hand movements and time can be saved!

    Q-Dir does not have to be installed and can be easily launched from the desktop.
    Rename Q-Dir_Installer.exe to Q-Dir.exe and DONE!!!.
    It’s a famous little utility for Windows OS and it’s also a friend of USB sticks ^_^.
    WARNING: Once Q-Dir, always Q-Dir ;)!
